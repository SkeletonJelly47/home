Car Gladiators Journey

After graduating from KUAS, I started looking for work and was very quickly picked up by the lovely folks at Dodreams, to work on the f2p title Drive Ahead!.

I was brought on to the Drive Ahead! team to work on new game modes, monetization and economy features. I started on UI programming and gradually started branching to client and server code. Working in a long lived code base has its challenges but it has been a great learning experience after almost exclusively working on brand new projects in my education.

After transitioning to a non-junior position, I broadened my skillset with improving our in-house LiveOps tools and managing our build pipelines. I'm grateful for all the good folks at Dodreams for fostering a great learning environment.
